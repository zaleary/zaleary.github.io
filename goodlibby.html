GoodLibby

Check your Goodreads to-read list against Libby.

Don't want to wait for Goodreads to export?

Take a list of books from anywhere and tell your favorite LLM to turn it into a CSV with three columns: Title, Author, and Bookshelves. Set Bookshelves to to-read for every row. Save it as a .csv file and upload it above.

Expected format:Title,Author,Bookshelves Neuromancer,William Gibson,to-read The Left Hand of Darkness,Ursula K. Le Guin,to-read Dune,Frank Herbert,to-read

Goodreads → My Books → Import and Export → Export Library.

Find yours at libbyapp.com/library/<slug>. Separate multiple with commas.
